Quarantine Paintings

All of these paintings were made during quarantine. I couldn't go out to buy canvases so I looked around the house for blank surfaces. I then found a bunch of old skateboards and I took off all of the trucks and grip tape from them. I also used cardboard from shipping boxes. I drew inspiration from dark surrealism and dark expressionism art pieces I found on the internet. Whenever I would spend time painting these, it would be therapeutic for me and I would never plan out a design.
