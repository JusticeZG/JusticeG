Quaranting Paintings

/ˈkwôrənˌtēn ˈpān(t)iNGs/

Time: Spring 2020

Project Type: Personal

Tools: Acrlyic Craft Paint

I painted these during the start of quarantine restrictions in Canada. I was feeling very lonely at the time since I wanted to spend the rest of my graduating year in highschool with my friends. I drew inspiration from dark surrealism and dark expressionism pieces I found on Instagram or Pinterest. I would never know what I would be painting whenever I started, I just painted whatever I thought looked cool. I also never used a canvas, because I couldn't find an art store that was open (which is why the paintings are on skateboard decks and a cardboard). Creating these paintings were an act of therapty for myself during these months.
