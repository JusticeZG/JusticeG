Quaranting Paintings

/ˈkwôrənˌtēn ˈpān(t)iNGs/

Time: Spring 2020

Project Type: Personal

Tools: Acrlyic Craft Paint

I painted these during the start of quarantine restrictions in Canada. I was feeling very lonely at the time since I wanted to spend the rest of my graduating year in high school with my friends. I drew inspiration from dark surrealism and dark expressionism pieces I found on Instagram or Pinterest. I would never know what I would be painting whenever I started, I just painted whatever I thought looked cool. I also never used a canvas, because I couldn't find an art store that was open (which is why the paintings are on skateboard decks and cardboard). Creating these paintings was an act of therapy for me during these months.
