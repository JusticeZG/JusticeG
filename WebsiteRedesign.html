Website Redesign

During the winter break, I was visiting different websites that my parents use regularly. One of them being my mom's Business Website. As I was going through the different features and interfaces I found that I didn't enjoy the experience. So, I decided to redesign her website as a fun side project. I also used this opportunity to learn Figma, an application I have been meaning to learn for a while.

The only feature on the website was the ability to book an appointment, everything else was information about the clinic (for example services, the staff, location, e.t.c). So I decided to create a mobile version of the website that focuses on this feature.

During the whole process, I asked my mom for feedback and she gave occasional feedback like the accessibility of some features and general design choices that she would want on her website.

As this is my first layout design, I am satisfied with the end product. I only planned to redesign this during the first two weeks of my winter break so I did not end up making a heavily detailed prototype.
