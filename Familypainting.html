Family Paintings

/ˈfam(ə)lē ˈpān(t)iNGs/

Time: Fall 2019

Project Type: School

Tools: Acrylic Paint

I was in an art program throughout middle school and highschool called CyberArts and I painted this piece and another in a set for a project. Unfortunately I do not have a high-quality picture of the second piece, however I do have a picture of this one. Painting became something I thoroughly enjoy over recent years as therapeutic break from everything else that happens in my life, and I this painting (along with the other half of the set) was the first to develop that taste.
