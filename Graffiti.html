Graffiti Design

/ɡrəˈfēdē dəˈzīn/

Time: Fall 2019

Project Type: School

Tools: Adobe Illustrator

I was assigned this project after I visited Graffiti Alley in Toronto with my Tech Design class in high school. We were required to create a graffiti design that spells out our name or nickname. I chose a name I use for online multiplayer games.
