Hello!

I'm Justice, a 2nd-year student at the University of Waterloo, studying Global Business and Digital Arts.

My design journey began when I joined the CyberArts program at my high school. There, I learned how to use Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, and Adobe Indesign. The program helped to build a strong foundation of skills that I use now for painting, drawing, visual design, and brand design.

I'm currently a part-time Visual Designer at Royaltymine in a part-time position, designing graphics, websites, mobile apps, and brand design.

While I'm aspiring to become a UX Designer in the future, I enjoy learning creative and design skills in ways that aren't related to web design and user research. I enjoy creative freedom and learning many different things has helped me broaden my perspectives on many projects.
